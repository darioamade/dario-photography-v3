About

A passion that evolved into landscape photography as a means of recording my travels and experience. I began to photograph the beauty around me to share with my family and friends back home in Portugal. My love of nature and quickly evolved into my true passion, landscape photography. I'm a landscape and travel photographer based in London.I start my journey years ago with cityscape and street photography but my drawn to mountains and waterfalls are more than anything .

Travelling and produce content about photography and film-making on my Youtube channel. My work has been published in a coffee table book, including Outdoor Photographer, Practical Photography and Cityscape. My images are all about the Outdoors and Nature and telling a story. The camera has truly changed my love for nature . Travelling with friends or by myself or even going to my local woodland....
I'm available today for hire, both nationally and internationally offering:

  • Outdoor Photography Education
  • Landscape Photography Workshops
  • Post Processing Education
  • Social Media Content Creation
